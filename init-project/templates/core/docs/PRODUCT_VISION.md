## 5W answers

- **Who:** primary user; buyer if different from user.
- **What:** the product in one sentence.
- **Why:** the pain or opportunity it addresses.
- **When:** first milestone, launch window, or target date.
- **Where:** channel and context of use (web, mobile, CLI, embedded, internal tool, etc.).
- **How (optional):** the core mechanism or approach in one line.
